Strawberry cake

Delicious, easy to make strawberry cake for 3 people

Ingrendients:

  1. Water
  2. Flour
  3. Sugar
  4. Eggs
  5. Strawberries

Equipment:

  1. Oven
  2. Knife
  3. Mixer
  4. Bowl

Instructions:

  1. Put flour in the bowl
  2. Slowly pour in water while mixing everything together
  3. Add 4 cups of sugar
  4. Crack in 2 eggs
  5. Bake in the oven
  6. Cut your strawberriess
  7. Sprinkle them on your baked cake
