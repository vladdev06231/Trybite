Homemade Sprite

Perfect for hot weathers and saving money.

Ingredients:

1.

Sparkling water

300ml

2.

Lemon

Half

3.

Lime

Half

4.

Sugar

2 tablespoons

Equipment:

1.

Lemon squeezer

Instructions:

1.

Squeeze your lemon and lime.

2.

Mix the lemon and the lime juice together.

3.

Pour sparkling water in your cup and pour the lime and lemon mixture in the water.

4.

Mix the sparkling water and the mixture together.

5.

Finish by adding 2-3 tablespoons of sugar into your drink and mix for 30 seconds.
